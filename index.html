About Text Sizes 

            

                

                    

                        
                    

                    
What is a Byte?

                    
A byte is the basic unit of digital information storage. One byte typically represents one character of text.

                

                

                    

                        
                    

                    
Storage in the 2000s

                    
In the early 2000s, storage was much more expensive. A megabyte of storage could cost around $1, compared to fractions of a cent today.

                

                

                    

                        
                    

                    
Text vs. Other Media

                    
Text is very efficient! A full novel might be just 1MB, while a single high-resolution photo could be 5MB or more.

                

                

                    

                        
                    

                    
Binary

                    
In computers, all text is ultimately stored as binary (0s and 1s). Each character is typically represented by 8 bits (1 byte).
